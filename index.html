Example Project: The Problem of Employee Attrition
A major tech company was losing valuable talent, with a costly **16%** annual attrition rate. The core question was: "Why are our best people leaving, and can we predict who is at risk?"
The Process: Uncovering Signals
I built a pipeline to analyze their HR dataset. By cleaning the data, engineering features like 'YearsSinceLastPromotion', and performing EDA, I began to isolate the key factors driving attrition.
The 'Aha!' Moment: Overtime is the Key
The analysis revealed a powerful insight. While many factors contributed, one stood out: employees working overtime had an attrition rate of **30.5%**, nearly **3 times higher** than those who didn't (10.4%).
The Impact: Proactive Retention
I delivered a Streamlit dashboard that allows managers to identify at-risk employees in real-time. This enables proactive conversations and support, leading to a significant potential reduction in talent loss.
